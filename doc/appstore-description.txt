App Name: Magnetic

Subtitle: Real-Time Metaball Visualizer

---

Description:

Magnetic is a mesmerizing real-time 3D metaball visualizer powered by Metal shaders.

Watch liquid metal shapes merge, split, and flow — all rendered in real time on your device. React to music through your microphone, or let the built-in BPM engine drive the motion.

FEATURES

- Real-time ray-marched 3D metaballs
- 5 material modes: Black, Mercury, Glass, Custom Color, Wireframe
- 12+ HDRI environment maps including studio, nature, and planetary textures
- Custom photo environment maps — use your own images
- Custom background colors with built-in color picker
- Audio-reactive mode with microphone input
- Manual BPM control for precise rhythm sync
- 9 orbit patterns: Random, Circle, Sphere, Torus, Spiral, Satellite, DNA, Figure-8, Wave
- Adjustable ball count, size, spacing, and orbit range
- Grid mode for structured arrangements
- Camera mode with automatic cinematic movement
- Double-tap to randomize patterns, colors, and configurations
- Screen recording to save visualizations
- Green screen mode for video compositing
- Minimal dark UI with full parameter control

Perfect as a live visual companion for music, a meditation tool, a creative screensaver, or just something beautiful to watch.
