Magnetic — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Overview

Magnetic is a real-time metaball visualization app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

Magnetic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

No analytics, no tracking, no accounts, no servers. Everything stays on your device.

Microphone Access

Magnetic may request microphone access to enable audio-reactive visualizations. Microphone audio is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings.

Photo Library Access

Magnetic may request photo library access to let you set custom environment maps and save recorded videos. Photos and videos are accessed and saved locally on your device. They are never uploaded or shared with any third party.

Third-Party Services

Magnetic does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Magnetic does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
